How to Track Prices on Amazon

Step 1: Find Your Product

Go to Amazon and search for the product you want to track. Copy the URL of the product page.

Step 2: Enter the Product URL

Paste the copied URL into the tracking form on our website.

Step 3: Set Your Budget

Enter your budget. We'll notify you when the price drops below this amount.

Step 4: Receive Notifications

Get instant notifications via email or Telegram when the price changes.
